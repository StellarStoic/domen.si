Who am I?

Yet my free time is expensive. It takes me some time to build and test, but I get things done eventually. The reason for this is 9-5 job where I'm far away from my beloved computer. Need a simple website? Find me on the internets.

Among all this, I'm still trying to take that Instagram photo that will go viral. Ha, just kidding!
